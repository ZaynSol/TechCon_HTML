Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Since then, it has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences in the world, hosting innovators, leaders, and pioneers from across the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire, educate, and connect people who are passionate about technology. We aim to foster innovation and collaboration, driving meaningful progress in the tech industry and beyond.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace is celebrated as the world’s first computer programmer. She has inspired generations with her vision of the power of computing.

Alan Turing

A pioneer in computer science and artificial intelligence, Alan Turing’s groundbreaking work laid the foundation for modern computing.

Grace Hopper

Rear Admiral Grace Hopper, known as the “Mother of COBOL,” revolutionized programming languages and made them accessible to the world.
